Easy Sour Cream Brownies!!!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Butter, melted
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 2 whole eggs (allow to come to room temperature)
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/3 cup cocoa
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup Sour Cream

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 350o F. Grease 8-in. square pan.
  2. In large mixing bowl blend butter, sugar and vanilla. Beat in room-temperature eggs.
  3. Using a spoon, mix in sour cream. Combine flour, cocoa, baking powder and salt.
  4. Stir dry ingredients mix into wet mix.
  5. Bake 35 minutes, check and possibly bake 5-10 minutes more.
  6. Cool for 10 â€“ 15 minutes.
