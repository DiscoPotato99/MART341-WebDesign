Drawing Instructions Instructable

Step 1 - Start with the basics

The basic outlines should always be the first step of your drawings. In this case i'm starting with the head of Ironman, but i always do the outline first and then refine the details later.

Step 2 - Branching Off

One you have a part of what you want, stretch that idea off and expand it one step at a time. Once i did the head then I do the body that's attached to it.

Step 3 - Finish Your Drawing

Once I drew the body I could then brach off to the arms and legs and finish the drawing how I wanted it to look. Sometimes doing the whole idea isn't how you want to represent it, in this case it was me not fully drawing the legs, but you can choose how you want your drawing to look.
